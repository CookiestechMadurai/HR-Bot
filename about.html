About Tutoapp

We're revolutionizing the way job seekers prepare for interviews through AI-powered mock interview experiences that provide personalized feedback and actionable insights.

Our Mission

At Tutoapp, we believe everyone deserves the opportunity to present their best self during job interviews. Our mission is to democratize interview preparation by providing accessible, personalized, and effective practice tools that help candidates build confidence and showcase their true potential.

Our Story

2023

The Idea

Tutoapp was born from a simple observation: despite having incredible skills, many talented individuals struggle to communicate effectively during interviews. Our founders experienced this firsthand while mentoring computer science graduates.

2024

Development & Beta Testing

We assembled a team of AI researchers, educational experts, and HR professionals to develop our first interview bot prototype. After months of training on thousands of real interview scenarios and feedback from beta testers, we refined our algorithms.

Early 2025

Official Launch

Tutoapp platform was officially launched with two specialized interview bots focusing on Computer Science and MBA candidates. Within the first three months, we helped over 50,000 users improve their interview performance.

Present

Growing & Evolving

Today, we're expanding our bot offerings to cover more industries and roles while continuously improving our algorithms to provide even more accurate and helpful feedback to our growing community.
